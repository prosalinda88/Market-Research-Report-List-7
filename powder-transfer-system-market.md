<p><p>The powder transfer system market includes three primary types: mechanical conveyors, pneumatic conveyors, and aviation machinery conveyors. Mechanical conveyors use moving parts, like belts or screws, to transport powders efficiently within a facility. Pneumatic conveyors utilize air pressure to move powders through tubes, providing flexibility and minimizing contamination. Aviation machinery conveyors are specifically designed for the aerospace industry, ensuring safe and efficient movement of powdered materials used in manufacturing and assembly processes, meeting stringent industry standards.</p></p>
