<p><strong>The Portable Mini Fridges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Compressor Refrigerating</li><li>Semiconductor Refrigeration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable mini fridge market features two main types: compressor refrigeration and semiconductor refrigeration. Compressor refrigerators utilize a mechanical compressor to circulate refrigerant, providing efficient cooling and a larger temperature range. They are suitable for long-term use and can maintain lower temperatures. On the other hand, semiconductor refrigerators rely on thermoelectric technology, using a heat pump to transfer heat, making them lighter and quieter but often less efficient. Each type caters to different consumer needs, ranging from mobility to energy efficiency.</p></p>
