<p><p>The fluorescent paint market is divided into two main types: organic and inorganic fluorescent paints. Organic fluorescent paints contain carbon-based compounds that produce vibrant colors and high brightness under ultraviolet light, often used in artistic applications and safety signage. Inorganic fluorescent paints, on the other hand, are derived from mineral compounds and are known for their durability and resistance to environmental factors. These are commonly utilized in industrial and outdoor settings, where longevity and visibility are critical.</p></p>
