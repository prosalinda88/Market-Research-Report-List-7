<p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) masks market comprises three main types: nasal pillow masks, nasal masks, and full face masks. Nasal pillow masks deliver air via small cushions that sit at the nostrils, providing a minimalistic and comfortable option. Nasal masks cover the nose entirely, offering a secure fit and suitable for varying sleeping positions. Full face masks encompass both the nose and mouth, accommodating users who may breathe through their mouths or require higher pressure settings for effective therapy.</p></p>
