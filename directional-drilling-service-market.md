<p><strong>The Directional Drilling Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional System</li><li>Rotary Steerable System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Directional Drilling Service market is divided into two main types: the Conventional System and the Rotary Steerable System. The Conventional System utilizes rotary drilling with the help of mud motors to create curved paths, allowing for precise well placement but requiring more manual intervention. In contrast, the Rotary Steerable System offers continuous rotation and real-time steering capabilities, enhancing accuracy and efficiency in drilling operations. This system reduces a reliance on downhole manipulation, streamlining drilling processes for complex well geometries.</p></p>
