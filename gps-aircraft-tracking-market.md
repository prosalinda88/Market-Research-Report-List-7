<p><ul><li>Portable GPS</li><li>Fixed GPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS aircraft tracking market is divided into two main types: portable GPS and fixed GPS. Portable GPS systems are lightweight and can be easily moved between aircraft, offering flexibility for operators who manage multiple planes. They often feature battery power and user-friendly interfaces. Conversely, fixed GPS systems are permanently installed in aircraft, providing continuous tracking and integration with other flight systems. These systems typically offer enhanced durability and reliability, making them ideal for consistent monitoring and data collection.</p></p>
