<p><p>Borazine, often referred to as "inorganic benzene," is a chemical compound with the formula B₃N₃H₆. It consists of a six-membered ring of alternating boron and nitrogen atoms, with hydrogen atoms attached to each. Borazine is known for its structural similarity to benzene, though it exhibits distinct chemical properties due to the difference in electronegativity between boron and nitrogen. It is primarily used in the production of boron nitride ceramics, which have applications in high-temperature materials, semiconductors, and coatings.</p><p>The Borazine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period.
