<p><ul><li>CTC Enrichment</li><li>CTC Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Circulating Tumor Cell (CTC) diagnostics market comprises two main types: CTC enrichment and CTC detection. CTC enrichment focuses on isolating and concentrating tumor cells from the bloodstream for analysis, enhancing the sensitivity of detection methods. On the other hand, CTC detection involves identifying and characterizing these isolated tumor cells using various techniques, such as molecular and genomic assays. Together, these technologies facilitate early cancer diagnosis, treatment monitoring, and assessment of metastatic potential in patients.</p></p>
