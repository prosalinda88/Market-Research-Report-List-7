Parfum, or extrait de parfum, has a higher concentration (15-30%), offering a more intense scent and longevity. Solid perfumes combine fragrance with a waxy base, providing a portable and convenient option for on-the-go application. Together, these formats cater to diverse consumer preferences and occasions.</p></p>
