<p><p>The marine incinerators market features two primary types: horizontal and vertical cyclone incinerators. Horizontal marine incinerators are designed for efficient waste combustion in a horizontal configuration, allowing for easier maintenance and operation. In contrast, vertical cyclone marine incinerators utilize a vertical design that enhances combustion efficiency through a cyclone effect, promoting better mixing and combustion of waste. Both types are crucial for managing shipboard waste responsibly while complying with environmental regulations at sea.</p></p>
