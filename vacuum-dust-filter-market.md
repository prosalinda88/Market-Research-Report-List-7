<p><p>The vacuum dust filter market is primarily categorized into wet filters and dry filters. Wet filters use a liquid medium to trap dust and particles, effectively capturing allergens and preventing them from recirculating in the air, making them ideal for industrial applications. Dry filters, on the other hand, use porous materials to capture dust without moisture, making them suitable for domestic and commercial use due to their efficiency and ease of maintenance. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing air quality.</p></p>
