<p><p>The lab goggles market can be categorized into three main types: PC (polycarbonate), PMMA (polymethyl methacrylate), and other materials. PC goggles are known for their high impact resistance and durability, making them suitable for demanding environments. PMMA goggles offer excellent optical clarity and are often lighter, but they may not be as impact-resistant as PC. Other materials encompass various alternatives, including glass and specialty polymers, catering to specific needs in laboratories and industrial settings, balancing factors like safety, comfort, and visibility.</p></p>
