<p><strong>The Peritoneal Dialysis Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD)</li><li>Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Peritoneal Dialysis Devices market encompasses two main types: Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD) and Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD). CAPD is a manual process, where patients perform dialysis several times a day, allowing for flexibility and independence. In contrast, APD utilizes a machine to automate the exchange process, typically performed overnight while the patient sleeps. Both methods effectively manage end-stage renal disease, catering to different patient preferences and lifestyles, thereby expanding access to dialysis treatment.</p></p>
