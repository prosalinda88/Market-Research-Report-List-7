<p><strong>The Dental Surgical Navigation Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>Electromagnetic (EM)</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental surgical navigation systems enhance precision during procedures by guiding surgeons in real-time. The market can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Optical navigation systems** utilize cameras and markers to track instruments' positions, providing visual feedback on the surgical site. </p><p>2. **Electromagnetic (EM) navigation systems** use electromagnetic fields to determine the position of tools within the mouth, allowing for accurate localization without line-of-sight restrictions. </p><p>3. **Hybrid systems** combine both optical and EM technologies, offering improved accuracy and flexibility in varied surgical environments.</p></p>
