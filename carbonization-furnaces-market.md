<p><p>Carbonization furnaces are specialized industrial equipment used to convert organic materials, such as biomass and coal, into carbon-rich char through a process known as carbonization. Carbonization furnaces find applications in various industries, including metallurgy, fuel production, and waste management, as they enable the efficient transformation of raw materials into valuable products.</p><p>The Carbonization Furnaces Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.8% during the forecast period.
