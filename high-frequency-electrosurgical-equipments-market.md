<p><strong>The High Frequency Electrosurgical Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Circuit</li><li>Bipolar Circuit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-frequency electrosurgical equipment is categorized into monopolar and bipolar circuits. Monopolar circuits use a single electrode to deliver radiofrequency energy, allowing for precise cutting and coagulation, often employed in larger surgical areas. In contrast, bipolar circuits utilize two electrodes, enabling energy to flow between them, which minimizes tissue damage and is ideal for smaller, delicate procedures. Both types enhance surgical efficiency and precision, making them essential in various medical applications.</p></p>
