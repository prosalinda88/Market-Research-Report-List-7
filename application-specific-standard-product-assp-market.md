<p><p>The Application Specific Standard Product (ASSP) market comprises various design types tailored for specific functions. Standard-cell designs utilize pre-defined logic cells for efficient layout and power management. Gate-array and semi-custom designs offer flexibility, enabling customization while maintaining standard manufacturing processes. Full-custom designs provide maximum optimization for performance and area but require significant resources. Structured designs combine elements of both custom and standard designs, aiming for scalability and shorter development times, making them suitable for diverse applications in electronic systems.</p></p>
