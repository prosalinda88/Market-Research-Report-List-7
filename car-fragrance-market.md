<p><p>The car fragrance market comprises three main types: aerosol, liquid, and solid. Aerosol fragrances are often spray-based, providing a quick and intense scent release, making them popular for immediate freshness. Liquid types typically involve a bottle or diffuser that gradually emits scent, offering a lasting aroma for long drives. Solid fragrances, often in gel or wax form, release fragrance slowly and can be placed in various locations within the car, providing a consistent but subtler scent experience.</p></p>
