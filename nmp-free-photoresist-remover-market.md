<p><p>The NMP-Free Photoresist Remover market is primarily divided into two types: positive photoresist remover and negative photoresist remover. Positive photoresist removers are designed to eliminate photoresist materials that become soluble upon exposure to light, allowing for selective area processing. Conversely, negative photoresist removers target photoresists that solidify upon exposure, requiring a different chemical approach for removal. Both types are crucial for semiconductor manufacturing and other applications where effective, environmentally friendly photoresist removal is essential.</p></p>
