<p><p>The washing and sterilizing machine market features two primary types: linear tunnel and rotary machines. Linear tunnel machines operate continuously, moving products through a series of washing and sterilizing stages, making them ideal for high-volume processing. In contrast, rotary machines use a circular motion to treat items, allowing for efficient cleaning in a compact footprint. Both technologies ensure effective sanitation and are essential in industries such as food processing and pharmaceuticals, where hygiene is critical.</p></p>
