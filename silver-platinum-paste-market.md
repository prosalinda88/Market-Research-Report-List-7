<p><p>The Silver-Platinum Paste market comprises two main types: curing paste and sintered paste. Curing paste is utilized for applications requiring a stable bond that sets through a chemical reaction, providing excellent conductivity and thermal resistance. In contrast, sintered paste involves a process where particles fuse together under heat, resulting in a durable and electrically conductive material often used in electronics and semiconductor manufacturing. Both types offer unique properties tailored to specific industrial applications, enhancing performance and reliability in various technologies.</p></p>
