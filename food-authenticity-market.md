<p><ul><li>PCR-Based</li><li>LC-MS/MS</li><li>Isotope</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Food authenticity market encompasses various analytical methods to verify the origin and composition of food products. PCR-based methods rely on genetic analysis to identify species and detect adulteration. LC-MS/MS (Liquid Chromatography-Tandem Mass Spectrometry) is used for chemical profiling and quantifying compounds, ensuring ingredient integrity. Isotope analysis measures the stable isotopic ratios in food, which can indicate geographical origin and production methods. These technologies enhance transparency and trust in food supply chains, addressing consumer concerns over authenticity.</p></p>
