<p><strong>The Automotive Ignition Coil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive ignition coil market includes two main types: single-spark and multi-spark systems. Single-spark ignition coils deliver one spark per cylinder per cycle, ensuring precise timing and reliable combustion. They are commonly used in traditional internal combustion engines. Multi-spark ignition coils generate multiple sparks in rapid succession during each cycle, improving combustion efficiency, reducing emissions, and enhancing engine performance. Multi-spark systems are increasingly adopted in modern vehicles for better fuel economy and compliance with stricter emission standards. Both types play a critical role in engine ignition systems.</p></p>
