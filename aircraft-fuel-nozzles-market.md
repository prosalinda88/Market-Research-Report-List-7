Evaporator tube nozzles facilitate efficient fuel evaporation before injection, enhancing performance. Oil throwing nozzles utilize mechanical motion to distribute fuel evenly, preventing engine hotspots. Other nozzles include specialized designs that cater to unique aircraft or engine requirements, ensuring optimal fuel delivery and efficiency across various aviation applications, thereby enhancing overall performance and safety.</p></p>
