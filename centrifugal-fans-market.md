<p><p>Centrifugal fans are categorized into three main types based on blade design: forward-curved, backward-curved, and radial. Forward-curved fans are efficient at lower pressures and provide high airflow, making them suitable for applications like ventilation. Backward-curved fans are efficient at higher pressures and are quieter, ideal for industrial settings. Radial fans offer a balanced performance, providing reliable airflow in various applications. Each type serves distinct operational needs in HVAC systems, industrial processes, and material handling.</p></p>
