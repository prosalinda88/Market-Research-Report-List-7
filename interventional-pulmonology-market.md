Bronchoalveolar lavage is useful for sampling lung fluid, and lung biopsy aids in tissue diagnosis. Bronchial stents support airway patency, and balloon bronchoplasty expands narrowed passages. Pleuroscopy offers insight into pleural diseases, with 'others' covering additional specialized techniques, enhancing patient care in respiratory medicine.</p></p>
