<p><ul><li>Desiccant Dryer</li><li>Refrigerated dryer</li><li>Membrane dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air dryer market comprises three main types: desiccant dryers, refrigerated dryers, and membrane dryers. Desiccant dryers utilize desiccant materials to absorb moisture, making them ideal for low-temperature applications. Refrigerated dryers cool compressed air to condense and remove moisture, suitable for general-purpose use. Membrane dryers employ selective permeable membranes to separate moisture from air, offering a compact solution for smaller systems. Each type serves different industrial needs, ensuring optimal air quality and equipment efficiency.</p></p>
