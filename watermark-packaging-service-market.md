<p><p>The Watermark Packaging Service Market is divided into two main types: visible watermark and invisible watermark. Visible watermarks are discernible marks printed on packaging that serve to deter counterfeiting and enhance brand recognition. They are often integrated into the design for aesthetic appeal. Conversely, invisible watermarks are embedded within the material and can only be detected using specific technology or tools, providing a discreet layer of security. Both types aim to protect intellectual property and maintain product authenticity in the marketplace.</p></p>
