<p><ul><li>External Heat Delivery System</li><li>Internal Heat Delivery System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hyperthermia systems market is categorized into two main types: external heat delivery systems and internal heat delivery systems. External systems involve external applicators that deliver heat to the skin's surface, often utilizing techniques like radiofrequency or microwave. In contrast, internal heat delivery systems target deeper tissues using methods such as intraluminal heating or perfusion techniques. Both systems aim to raise tissue temperature to enhance therapeutic effects, particularly in cancer treatment, by improving the efficacy of other therapies and targeting tumor cells.</p></p>
