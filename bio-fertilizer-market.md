<p><p>The biofertilizer market encompasses various types based on microbial sources. Bacterial fertilizers use nitrogen-fixing bacteria to enhance soil fertility, promoting plant growth. Actinomycetes fertilizers, derived from filamentous bacteria, improve soil health and nutrient availability through organic matter decomposition. Fungi fertilizers utilize mycorrhizal fungi to form symbiotic relationships with plant roots, enhancing nutrient absorption and resilience. Each type contributes to sustainable agriculture by reducing chemical inputs, improving soil quality, and supporting eco-friendly practices.</p></p>
