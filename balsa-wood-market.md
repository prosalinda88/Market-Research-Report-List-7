<p><p>Balsa wood is categorized into Grain A, Grain B, and Grain C based on its density and grain structure. Grain A is the lightest and softest, ideal for applications requiring minimal weight and easy shaping, like model building. Grain B offers a balance between weight and strength, suitable for structural components in lightweight projects. Grain C is the densest and strongest, used for applications needing durability and load-bearing capacity, such as marine or industrial uses. Each type serves specific market needs based on performance requirements.</p></p>
