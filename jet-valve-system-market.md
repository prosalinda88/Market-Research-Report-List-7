<p><strong>The Jet Valve System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Jet Valves</li><li>Non-contact Jet Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Jet Valve System market is segmented into two main types: contact jet valves and non-contact jet valves. Contact jet valves operate by maintaining physical contact with the workpiece, providing precise control over material deposition and often ensuring better adhesion. In contrast, non-contact jet valves utilize a pneumatic or electromagnetic mechanism to deliver materials without direct contact, offering advantages such as reduced wear and contamination risk. Both types cater to diverse applications in industries like manufacturing, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
