<p><ul><li>Steam</li><li>Dry Heat</li><li>EO</li><li>Hydrogen Peroxide</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chemical indicators market encompasses various types used in sterilization processes. Steam indicators change color to confirm exposure to steam during autoclaving. Dry heat indicators are designed for use in dry heat sterilization cycles. Ethylene oxide (EO) indicators are utilized for processes involving EO gas, ensuring proper sterilization. Hydrogen peroxide indicators are used in plasma sterilization, while "others" may include indicators for ozone or radiation sterilization methods. Each type is essential for verifying effective sterilization across medical and laboratory settings.</p></p>
