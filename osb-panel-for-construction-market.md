<p><p>OSB (Oriented Strand Board) panels are categorized into four types based on their structural properties and intended uses. OSB/1 is suitable for general internal applications, while OSB/2 is designed for load-bearing purposes in dry conditions. OSB/3 offers enhanced moisture resistance for use in humid environments, making it ideal for construction in varying climates. OSB/4 is the strongest option, engineered for high-load applications and exposed conditions, ensuring durability and performance in demanding construction scenarios.</p></p>
