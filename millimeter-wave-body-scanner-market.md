<p><p>The millimeter wave body scanner market consists of two primary types: flat scanning and cylindrical scanning. Flat scanning involves a stationary flat panel that captures images of a person's body, typically used in security settings like airports. In contrast, cylindrical scanning utilizes a rotating antenna system that creates a three-dimensional image by capturing data from multiple angles around the person. Both technologies serve to enhance security by detecting concealed items while ensuring minimal exposure and privacy concerns for individuals being scanned.</p></p>
