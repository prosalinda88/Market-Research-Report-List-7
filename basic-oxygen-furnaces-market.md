<p><p>The Basic Oxygen Furnaces (BOF) market includes two main types: Blast Furnace - Basic Oxygen Furnace (BF-BOF) and Electric Arc Furnace (EAF). The BF-BOF method utilizes molten iron from blast furnaces and converts it into steel through the injection of oxygen, ensuring high efficiency for large-scale production. In contrast, EAF primarily uses recycled scrap steel and electric arcs for melting, offering flexibility and lower emissions, thus catering to smaller production needs and increasing demand for sustainable practices in steelmaking.</p></p>
