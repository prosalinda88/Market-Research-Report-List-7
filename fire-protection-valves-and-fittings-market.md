<p><ul><li>Wet System Valves</li><li>Dry System Valves</li><li>Deluge & Pre-Action System Valves</li><li>General Purpose Valves (Pressure Regulating Valve, Butterfly Valve, Trim, Fail Safe, Check Valve)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire protection valves and fittings market comprises various types designed for specific fire suppression systems. Wet system valves control water flow in constant wet pipe systems, while dry system valves operate in systems where pipes are filled with pressurized air until activated. Deluge and pre-action system valves manage water release in response to fire detection. General purpose valves include pressure regulating valves for maintaining optimal pressure, butterfly valves for flow control, trim valves for system components, fail-safe valves ensuring reliable operation, and check valves preventing backflow.</p></p>
