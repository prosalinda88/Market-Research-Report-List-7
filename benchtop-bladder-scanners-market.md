<p><p>Benchtop bladder scanners are devices used to measure bladder volume non-invasively. The market is divided into two main types: 2D and 3D scanners. 2D scanners provide a flat image and calculate volume based on two-dimensional dimensions, making them simpler and often more affordable. In contrast, 3D scanners create a comprehensive three-dimensional image of the bladder, offering greater accuracy and detailed spatial information. This distinction influences their applications in urology, geriatrics, and other medical fields where bladder assessment is crucial.</p></p>
