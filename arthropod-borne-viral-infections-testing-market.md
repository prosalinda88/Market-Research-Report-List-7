<p><p>The Arthropod-borne Viral Infections Testing Market includes various testing methods designed to diagnose viral infections transmitted by arthropods. ELISA-based tests detect antibodies or antigens through enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays, allowing for sensitive and specific identification. RT-PCR-based tests utilize reverse transcription polymerase chain reaction to detect viral RNA, offering rapid results and high accuracy. Immunochromatography rapid diagnosis tests provide quick screening using a simple lateral flow format, enabling immediate results, which is critical for timely clinical decision-making in outbreak management.</p></p>
