<p><ul><li>Electro-hydraulic</li><li>Electric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Point motor market types include electro-hydraulic and electric systems, each serving distinct applications in railway operations. Electro-hydraulic point motors utilize hydraulic pressure to operate switch mechanisms, offering high force and reliability, particularly in heavy-traffic areas. In contrast, electric point motors rely on electrical energy to actuate the switch points, providing quicker response times and reduced maintenance. Both technologies enhance safety and efficiency in rail systems, with choices depending on specific operational needs and infrastructure constraints.</p></p>
