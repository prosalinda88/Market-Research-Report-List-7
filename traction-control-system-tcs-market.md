<p><ul><li>Braking Torque Control</li><li>Engine Torque Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Traction Control System (TCS) enhances vehicle stability by preventing wheel spin during acceleration. The market is divided into two primary types: Braking Torque Control and Engine Torque Control. Braking Torque Control uses brakes to reduce wheel slip by applying force to the spinning wheels, ensuring better traction. Engine Torque Control, on the other hand, adjusts engine power output to maintain optimal traction without wheel slip. Both types work in tandem to improve vehicle safety and performance on various surfaces.</p></p>
