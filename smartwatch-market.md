<p><p>The smartwatch market comprises three main types: Companion Smartwatches, Standalone Smartwatches, and Classic Smartwatches. Companion Smartwatches rely on smartphones for functionality, offering notifications and fitness tracking while needing tethering. Standalone Smartwatches operate independently, featuring built-in cellular capabilities, apps, and storage for music, allowing users to stay connected without a phone. Classic Smartwatches emphasize traditional watch design, combining analog aesthetics with modern technology, appealing to those seeking a sophisticated look while still providing essential smartwatch features.</p></p>
