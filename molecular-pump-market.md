<p><p>The molecular pump market consists of several types, including turbo molecular pumps, combined molecular pumps, and molecular drag pumps. Turbo molecular pumps utilize high-speed rotors to achieve low pressures, ideal for vacuum applications. Combined molecular pumps integrate features of different pump types for enhanced performance across varied conditions. Molecular drag pumps harness the momentum of gas molecules and are effective in ultra-high vacuum environments. Each type serves specific industrial and research applications, contributing to advancements in vacuum technology.</p></p>
