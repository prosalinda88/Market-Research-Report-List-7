<p><p>The external bone growth stimulators market includes three primary types: Combined Magnetic Field (CMF) devices utilize both low-frequency pulsed electromagnetic fields and a constant magnetic field to promote bone healing; Capacitive Coupling (CC) devices employ an electrical field to stimulate cellular activity and enhance bone regeneration; and Pulsed Electromagnetic Field (PEMF) devices generate low-frequency electromagnetic fields to promote cellular repair and reduce inflammation. Each type aims to accelerate the healing process in fractures and orthopedic surgeries.</p></p>
