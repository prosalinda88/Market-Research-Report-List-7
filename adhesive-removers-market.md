<p><ul><li>Citrus Based</li><li>Soy Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Adhesive removers are categorized into three main types: citrus-based, soy-based, and solvent-based. Citrus-based removers leverage natural ingredients from citrus fruits, providing effective removal with a pleasant scent. Soy-based options use soy protein as a primary ingredient, making them eco-friendly and less harsh on surfaces. Solvent-based removers often contain chemicals that dissolve adhesives efficiently, suitable for tougher jobs but may have stronger odors. Each type serves different needs, balancing effectiveness with environmental and safety considerations.</p></p>
