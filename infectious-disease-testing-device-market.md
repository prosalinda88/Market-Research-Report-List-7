<p><p>The infectious disease testing device market encompasses various types of diagnostic methods. Molecular diagnostic tests utilize techniques like PCR to detect specific genetic material from pathogens, offering high sensitivity and specificity. Point-of-Care Testing (POCT) devices provide rapid results at the site of patient care, enhancing timely decision-making. Immunodiagnostic tests leverage antibodies to identify infectious agents, including enzyme-linked immunosorbent assays (ELISA) and rapid tests, allowing for effective screening and diagnosis in diverse healthcare settings. Each type plays a crucial role in managing infectious diseases.</p></p>
