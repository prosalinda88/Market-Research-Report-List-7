<p><strong>The ICU-acquired Acute Kidney Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-renal</li><li>Intrinsic-renal</li><li>Post-renal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ICU-acquired acute kidney treatment market can be categorized into three types: pre-renal, intrinsic-renal, and post-renal. Pre-renal conditions arise from inadequate blood flow to the kidneys, often due to dehydration or shock. Intrinsic-renal issues stem from direct damage to kidney tissue, typically caused by toxins, medications, or infections. Post-renal conditions involve obstructions in the urinary tract, leading to fluid backup and kidney damage. Each category requires specific treatment approaches, influencing market dynamics and product demand.</p></p>
