<p><p>The OLED market is categorized into three types: monochrome, multi-color, and full-color displays. Monochrome OLEDs produce a single color, often used in simple applications like digital clocks and indicators due to their efficiency and clarity. Multi-color OLEDs enable a range of colors but are less complex than full-color displays, finding applications in devices like smartphones and signage. Full-color OLEDs offer vibrant, high-resolution images, widely used in televisions and monitors, providing superior color accuracy and viewing angles.</p></p>
