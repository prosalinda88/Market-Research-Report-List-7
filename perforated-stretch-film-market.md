<p><p>The perforated stretch film market offers two main types: perforated manual film and perforated machine film. Perforated manual film is designed for hand application, featuring small holes that allow for air circulation and moisture release, making it ideal for wrapping pallets and perishable goods. Perforated machine film, on the other hand, is used in automatic wrapping machines and is engineered for high-speed operations. Both types enhance efficiency in packaging and storage while preserving product integrity during transport.</p></p>
