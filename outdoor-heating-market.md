Tabletop heaters are compact units designed to fit on tables, offering warmth for smaller spaces or dining settings. Mountable heaters are fixed to walls or ceilings, providing efficient heat without occupying ground space, making them suitable for balconies or outdoor dining areas. Together, these options enhance outdoor comfort year-round.</p></p>
