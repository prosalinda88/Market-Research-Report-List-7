<p><p>Particle Enhanced Turbidimetric Immunoassay (PETIA) is an advanced diagnostic technique that utilizes the principles of turbidimetry to measure the concentration of specific antigens or antibodies in a biological sample. It enhances the traditional turbidimetric method by using particulate matter, such as colored latex beads, which improve the sensitivity and specificity of the assay. This method offers faster results and high precision, making it suitable for various clinical diagnostics including infectious diseases, autoimmune disorders, and cancer markers.</p><p>The PETIA market has been experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, the rising demand for point-of-care testing, and advancements in assay technologies.
