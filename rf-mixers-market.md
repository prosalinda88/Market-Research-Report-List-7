<p><p>RF mixers are essential components in wireless communication systems, used to combine or separate frequencies. The market is divided into **Active Mixers** and **Passive Mixers**. **Active Mixers** incorporate amplifiers, offering higher gain, better isolation, and lower noise, making them ideal for applications requiring signal amplification. **Passive Mixers** rely on passive components like diodes or transformers, providing lower power consumption, higher linearity, and robustness, suitable for high-frequency and high-power applications. Both types cater to diverse needs in telecommunications, radar, and IoT, balancing performance, cost, and power efficiency.</p></p>
