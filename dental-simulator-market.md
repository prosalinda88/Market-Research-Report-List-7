<p><strong>The Dental Simulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Workstation</li><li>Upper Body</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental simulator market is divided into two main types: Workstation and Upper Body simulators. Workstation simulators offer a comprehensive setup that includes a dental chair, tools, and simulation software, enabling realistic training for dental procedures. In contrast, Upper Body simulators focus on anatomical training, replicating the head and neck region, allowing for detailed practice of techniques like injections and assessments. Both types provide essential hands-on experience for dental students and professionals, enhancing clinical skills and confidence in real-life scenarios.</p></p>
