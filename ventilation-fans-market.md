<p><p>The ventilation fans market comprises three main types: ceiling fans, window-mounted fans, and wall-mounted fans. Ceiling fans circulate air in large spaces, enhancing comfort and reducing reliance on air conditioning. Window-mounted fans improve cross-ventilation by drawing in fresh air or exhausting indoor air, ideal for smaller areas. Wall-mounted fans save floor space and can effectively cool specific zones in homes or businesses. Each type serves unique ventilation needs, contributing to efficient airflow management and climate control in various environments.</p></p>
