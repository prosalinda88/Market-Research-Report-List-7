<p><p>Enterprise Asset Management (EAM) tools are categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based EAM solutions are hosted off-site, offering scalability, remote accessibility, and reduced IT maintenance costs. They enable real-time data access and collaboration across locations. On-premises EAM tools, however, are installed locally on a company’s infrastructure, providing greater control over data security and customization. While they may require more upfront investment and maintenance, they cater to organizations with specific compliance and operational needs.</p></p>
