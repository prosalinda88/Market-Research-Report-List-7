<p><p>Guided Wave Radar (GWR) liquid level sensors come primarily in two types: probe type and cable type. Probe type sensors feature a solid rod that transmits radar signals directly, ideal for measuring levels in smaller tanks or vessels. In contrast, cable type sensors utilize a flexible cable that can span larger distances, making them suitable for tall tanks or complex installations. Both types offer high accuracy and reliability in various industrial applications, providing essential data for level measurement and monitoring.</p></p>
