<p><p>Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) facilitates the charging of electric vehicles and is categorized into four levels. Level 1 uses standard household outlets (120V) for slow charging, ideal for home use. Level 2 employs a 240V outlet, offering faster charging suitable for both home and public stations. Level 3, or DC fast charging, provides rapid charging for commercial sites, significantly reducing downtime. Level 4, still under development, aims for ultra-fast charging capabilities, enhancing convenience for long-distance travel.</p></p>
