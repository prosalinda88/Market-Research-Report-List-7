<p><p>Pressure spray dryers are specialized industrial equipment used to convert liquid solutions into powder form through the application of heat and pressure. This process involves atomizing a liquid feed into small droplets, which are then subjected to a heated gas, leading to rapid evaporation of moisture and the formation of dry particles.
