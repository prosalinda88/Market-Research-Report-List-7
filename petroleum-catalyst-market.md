Hydrotreating catalysts remove impurities to improve fuel quality, while hydrocracking catalysts facilitate the breakdown of large molecules into lighter products. Catalytic reforming catalysts upgrade naphtha into high-octane fuels. Additionally, 'Others' encompass specialized catalysts for niche applications, contributing to overall efficiency and effectiveness in petroleum refining processes.</p></p>
