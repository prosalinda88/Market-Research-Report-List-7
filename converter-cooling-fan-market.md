<p><p>The Converter Cooling Fan market is categorized into two main types: air-cooled and water-cooled systems. Air-cooled fans use ambient air to dissipate heat, making them simpler and easier to maintain, ideal for environments where water supply is limited. In contrast, water-cooled systems circulate water to absorb heat, providing more efficient cooling in high-performance applications. Each type caters to different operational needs, with air-cooled fans suited for lower thermal demands and water-cooled fans designed for heavy-duty usage in critical applications.</p></p>
