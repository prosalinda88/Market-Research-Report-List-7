<p><p>Automatic License Plate Recognition (ALPR/ANPR) is a technology that uses optical character recognition (OCR) and image processing to automatically detect and read vehicle license plates. It is widely used in traffic management, law enforcement, toll collection, parking management, and security systems. ALPR systems capture images of license plates, extract the alphanumeric characters, and match them against databases for various applications, such as identifying stolen vehicles, enforcing traffic laws, or automating access control.</p><p>The Automatic License Plate Recognition (ALPR/ANPR) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period.
