<p><ul><li>Forced Resonance Analyzers</li><li>Free Resonance Analyzers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dynamic Mechanical Analyzers (DMA) are instruments used to study the mechanical properties of materials as a function of temperature, time, frequency, and applied stress. The market is divided into two main types: Forced Resonance Analyzers and Free Resonance Analyzers. Forced Resonance Analyzers apply an external force to vibrate the sample at a specific frequency, providing detailed insights into material behavior. In contrast, Free Resonance Analyzers rely on the natural vibration of the sample, offering different insights into its viscoelastic properties.</p></p>
