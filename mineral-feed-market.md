<p><p>The mineral feed market is divided into two main types: macro minerals and micro minerals. Macro minerals include essential elements like calcium, phosphorus, magnesium, and sodium, which are required in larger quantities for animal health, growth, and production. Micro minerals, on the other hand, encompass trace elements like zinc, copper, iron, and selenium, needed in smaller amounts but are crucial for various physiological functions. Both categories play a vital role in livestock nutrition, enhancing performance and overall well-being.</p></p>
