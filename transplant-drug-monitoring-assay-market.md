<p><ul><li>Chromatography-MS</li><li>Immunoassays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transplant drug monitoring assay market is primarily categorized into two types: chromatography-mass spectrometry (MS) and immunoassays. Chromatography-MS combines separation techniques with mass spectrometry, offering high sensitivity and specificity for measuring drug levels, making it ideal for complex biological samples. Immunoassays utilize antibodies to detect specific drugs, providing rapid and cost-effective results. Both methods play a crucial role in ensuring optimal medication levels for transplant patients, enhancing safety and efficacy in post-transplant care.</p></p>
