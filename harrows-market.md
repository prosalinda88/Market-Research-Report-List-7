<p><strong>The Harrows Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disk Harrow</li><li>Tine Harrow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Harrows are essential agricultural implements designed for soil preparation and seedbed preparation. The disk harrow features sharp, round blades that efficiently cut through and break up soil, making it suitable for tougher conditions. In contrast, the tine harrow uses long, pointed tines to aerate and level soil, beneficial for finer seedbed establishment. Both types are crucial in enhancing soil structure, promoting better crop yields, and facilitating effective seed germination, catering to various agricultural needs and soil types.</p></p>
