<p><p>The Next Generation Sequencers market encompasses various technologies. Sequencing by Synthesis (SBS) uses fluorescently labeled nucleotides to reveal DNA sequences during amplification. Ion Semiconductor Sequencing detects pH changes that occur during nucleotide incorporation, enabling real-time data collection. Single-Molecule Real-time (SMRT) Sequencing allows for the observation of DNA polymerases at work, providing real-time insights without amplification. Other market types may include technologies like nanopore sequencing and advanced optical methods, contributing to a diverse landscape of genomic sequencing techniques.</p></p>
