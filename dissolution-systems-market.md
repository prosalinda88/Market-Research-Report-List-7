<p><p>Dissolution systems are critical in pharmaceuticals for assessing drug release from formulations. The market is segmented into two types: offline and online systems. Offline dissolution systems involve batch testing in controlled environments, providing detailed, precise measurements of solubility and release profiles but requiring manual intervention for analysis. Conversely, online dissolution systems facilitate real-time monitoring and data collection during the dissolution process, enhancing efficiency and reducing human error, ultimately leading to faster decision-making in formulation development and quality control.</p></p>
