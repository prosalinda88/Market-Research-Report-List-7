<p><p>The Reverse Shoulder Arthroplasty (RSA) market comprises several key types, primarily Total Shoulder Arthroplasty (TSA) and Ream & Run procedures. TSA involves replacing damaged shoulder joint surfaces with artificial components, aimed at restoring function and alleviating pain. In contrast, the Ream & Run technique focuses on reshaping the joint surfaces without using a prosthesis, encouraging the body to use its own structures for stability. Both methods cater to different patient needs and conditions, enhancing shoulder mobility and quality of life.</p></p>
