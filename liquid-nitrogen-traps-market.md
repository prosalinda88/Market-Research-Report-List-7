<p><p>The liquid nitrogen traps market features two primary types: inline and right angle traps. Right angle traps, on the other hand, are positioned at a 90-degree angle, offering flexibility in installation and maintaining optimal flow paths. Both types serve crucial roles in processes requiring low temperatures, ensuring effective separation and preservation of materials in various industrial and laboratory settings.</p></p>
