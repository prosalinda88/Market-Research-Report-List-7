Planar protein assays utilize flat surfaces for simultaneous analysis of multiple proteins, offering high sensitivity. Bead-based protein assays employ microbeads coated with specific capture antibodies, enabling the detection of various proteins in a single sample through flow cytometry. Other protein assays may incorporate innovative techniques such as microfluidics or imaging approaches, catering to diverse research and diagnostic applications in proteomics.</p></p>
