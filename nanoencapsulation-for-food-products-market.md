Market types include spray congealing, which forms particles by rapidly cooling a melted blend; fluid bed coating, where particles are coated in a fluidized bed for uniform distribution; wax and lipid coatings, providing barriers for moisture and oxidation; and melt extrusion, where materials are combined under heat and pressure.
