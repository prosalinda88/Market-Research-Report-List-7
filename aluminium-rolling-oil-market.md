<p><strong>The Aluminium Rolling Oil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Rolling</li><li>Hot Rolling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminium rolling oil market comprises two main types: cold rolling and hot rolling. Cold rolling oil is used in processes that deform aluminium at room temperature, providing lubrication and cooling to enhance surface finish and reduce friction. In contrast, hot rolling oil is employed in high-temperature processes where aluminium is shaped while heated, facilitating easier metal flow and minimizing oxidation. Both types are crucial for optimizing manufacturing efficiency and ensuring the quality of the final aluminium products.</p></p>
