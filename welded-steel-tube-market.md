<p><strong>The Welded Steel Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Straight Seam Welded Pipe</li><li>Spiral Welded Pipe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welded steel tube market is primarily categorized into two types: straight seam welded pipes and spiral welded pipes. Straight seam welded pipes are produced by welding flat strips together in a longitudinal manner, offering high strength and uniform wall thickness, making them ideal for structural applications. In contrast, spiral welded pipes are formed by wrapping steel strips in a spiral configuration, allowing for larger diameters and versatility in manufacturing, making them suitable for various fluid transportation and industrial uses.</p></p>
