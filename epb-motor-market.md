<p><p>The EPB (Electric Parking Brake) motor market primarily features two types: DC Brushed Motors and DC Brushless Motors. DC Brushed Motors use brushes for commutation, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but with higher maintenance due to brush wear. DC Brushless Motors, on the other hand, use electronic commutation, providing higher efficiency, longer lifespan, and reduced maintenance, albeit at a higher cost. Both are integral to EPB systems, with brushless motors increasingly favored for their durability and performance in modern automotive applications.</p></p>
