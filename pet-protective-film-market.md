<p><p>The PET protective film market encompasses various types based on viscosity and adhesive properties. Micro-adhesive protective films offer light adhesion for easy removal, while low viscosity films provide moderate stickiness for various applications. Medium viscosity films balance adhesion and removability, whereas high viscosity films ensure strong bonding for durable protection. Ultra high viscosity films cater to demanding applications requiring maximum adhesion. Each type serves distinct needs in industries like electronics, automotive, and construction, ensuring surface protection during handling and transportation.</p></p>
