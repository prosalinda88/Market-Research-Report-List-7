<p><ul><li>Vertical Type</li><li>Horizontal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rubber injection molding machines are categorized into vertical and horizontal types based on their configuration and operational mechanics. Vertical injection molding machines are designed with the injection unit positioned vertically, optimizing space and facilitating easy part removal, making them ideal for small, intricate components. In contrast, horizontal machines feature a horizontal injection unit and are used extensively for larger products, offering faster cycle times and higher production rates. Both types cater to different production needs in the rubber industry.</p></p>
